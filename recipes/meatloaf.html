Easy meatloaf

This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf. It won't take long to make at all, and it's all quite good!

Ingredients

  • 1 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup ketchup

Steps

  4. Bake at 175 degrees for 1 hour.
